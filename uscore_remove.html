U-Score Remove Account Instructions



Note: The app ONLY stores your name and nickname so other players can find games you've hosted.

  1. Sign-in to the app.
  2. Navigate to Home
  3. Tap the profile icon.
  4. Click on the link "Remove account...".
  5. When asked, enter your existing password and press okay.
  6. The app will erase your data and delete your account.
